📋 Exam Rules

  • Question sampling: Each attempt picks 40 questions at random from a bank of 114, weighted by module exam-weight. Two attempts will not see identical questions.
  • Navigation: You can move between questions freely. Flag questions to revisit. The sidebar shows answered (orange), unanswered (white), and flagged (amber) states.
  • Timer: 45 minutes from when you click Start. Timer turns amber under 5 minutes, red under 1 minute, and the exam auto-submits at zero.
  • Attempts: 3 attempts maximum. Highest score is recorded. Pass on any attempt and you're certified.
  • Resources: No external resources, screen sharing, or AI assistants. The exam is monitored via the SF Knowledge Hub proctoring flow.
  • Submission: Submit early via the green button in the sidebar. Unanswered questions count as wrong.
